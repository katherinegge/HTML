  • One side of a volleyball court measures about 60 feet by 30 feet.
  • It is split up into 6 different zones, each usually occupied by their respective position.
  • The volleyball net is centered between each side; neither side can cross the net to the other side.
  • A 10 foot line on each side separates front-row players and back-row players.

Rules of Volleyball 🏐

  1. There must be 6 players on the court at all times, 3 front-row and 3 back-row players.
  2. Each side is allowed 3 touches, an individual player may not touch the ball twice in succession.
  3. Hitting the ball out is an immediate point for the other side.
    4. - The ball hits ground outside of the court
    - The ball hits the antennae
    - The ball hits the poles or the ref pole
    - The ball hits the ceiling
  4. It is illegal to catch, throw, or hold the ball for too long.
  5. Players earn points by grounding the ball on the other side. Each set is played to 25 points.

Rule Variations

Men's Volleyball
  • The men's net height is 7 feet 11 inches.
  • Men's volleyball is a more power-driven game.
  • The typical male volleyball player has a jump topspin serve.
Womens Volleyball
  • The women's net height is 7 feet 4 inches.
  • Women's volleyball is a game of precision and strategy.
  • The typical female volleyball player has a jump float serve.
