One side of a volleyball court measures about 60 feet by 30 feet. It is split up into 6 different zones, each usually occupied by their respective position. The volleyball net is centered between each side of the court; a women's net is 7'4" while a men's net is 7'11". A 10 foot line on each side separates front-row players and back-row players.
